> In loving memory of William Paterson who died 26 April 1906 aged 84 years.
>
> Also his beloved wife Barbara MacKenzie who died 27 April 1884 aged 62 years.

> "Till the day break"
>
> <br/>
>
> Erected by their daughters Barbara and Mina.
